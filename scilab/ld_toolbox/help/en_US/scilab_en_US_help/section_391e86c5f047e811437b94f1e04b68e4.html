I2C

  • ld_I2CDevice_BufferWrite — Raw write of vecsize bytes to an I2C-bus.
  • ld_I2CDevice_Read — read one byte from a register of an I2C-device
  • ld_I2CDevice_Transmit — Transmit / Flush write buffers and send the collected data to the device.
  • ld_I2CDevice_Write — Write to a one byte register of an I2C-device
  • ld_I2CDevice_shObj — I2C device connection
