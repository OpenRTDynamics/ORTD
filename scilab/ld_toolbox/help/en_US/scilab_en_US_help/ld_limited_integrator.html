ld_limited_integrator

Implements a time discrete integrator with saturation of the output between min__ and max__

Calling Sequence

[sim, y] = ld_limited_integrator(sim, ev, u, min__, max__, Ta)

Description

u * - input

y * - output

y(k+1) = sat( y(k) + Ta*u , min__, max__ )
